[NVIDIA Developer] Enhancing Dedalord’s Skiing Fred for Tegra 4

October 29, 2013 · 1 min · 63 words

One of the first games I worked on at Dedalord was Skiing Fred, a fast paced mobile game runner where I worked on a lot of the graphics and some of the gameplay.

The Tegra 4 had just released, so we implemented some additional graphical features with the additional horsepower that it provided.
